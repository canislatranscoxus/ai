**Flashcard 1:** 
**Front:** What did Jupiter initially send to the frogs as their king?
**Back:** A big Log that fell into the lake with a splash, causing the Frogs to hide in fear.

**Flashcard 2:** 
**Front:** What were the frogs' reactions when they saw the Stork walking down to the lake?
**Back:** They were delighted and welcomed him as their new king, not realizing his true nature.

**Flashcard 3:** 
**Front:** What was the consequences of the frogs accepting the Stork as their king?
**Back:** The Stork ate them up for breakfast, dinner, and supper every day, ultimately leading to the extinction of all Frogs in the lake.

**Flashcard 4:** 
**Front:** How did the Stork's appearance react with the frogs' expectations of a king?
**Back:** He was seen as grand and majestic at first, but later revealed his carnivorous nature.

**Flashcard 5:** 
**Front:** What lesson can be learned from the story of the Frogs and their King?
**Back:** Be content with what you have now, for it's often better than seeking something that might not turn out to be as good.
